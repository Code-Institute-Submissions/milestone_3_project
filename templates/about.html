About Us

Who we are?

We are a group of SEND teachers and Speech and Language Therapist trying to share free resources and links to support students with Speech, Language and Communication Needs.

What we offer?

A free resource that helps you to find training and interventions to meet your students needs.

What does it cost?

Nothing. We are doing this to help promote awareness of all the wonderful training and interventions out there. However, some training and interventions may require cost.

How will it help my school?

School staff can immediately search, or review (or hopefully add their own) training and interventions that can be used within their school. This will save you time, instead of thinking "what can I do?" or "How do I meet these needs?" we have collected it all for you (and continue to grow).
